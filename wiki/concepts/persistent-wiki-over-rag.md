# Persistent Wiki over RAG

## Definition
A knowledge workflow where the LLM continuously compiles source material into maintained markdown pages, rather than only retrieving raw chunks at answer time.

## Why It Matters
It enables cumulative synthesis, durable cross-references, and explicit contradiction handling that improve with each ingest.

## Evidence
The source argues that per-query retrieval repeatedly reconstructs context, while a maintained wiki preserves prior synthesis.
