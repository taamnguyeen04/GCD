# Index vs Log

## Definition
`index.md` is a content-oriented catalog; `log.md` is a chronological record of operations.

## Why It Matters
Separating navigation from chronology improves retrieval and operational traceability.

## Evidence
The source explicitly distinguishes use-cases: `index.md` for finding topics/pages, `log.md` for timeline and recent actions.
